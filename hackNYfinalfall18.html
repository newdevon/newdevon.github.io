Scan

This page contains a scanning button which can open up a search box on your computer which you can use to search for the downloaded image of the QR code. After scanning it through a camera-enabled device, it will take you to the profile of the person you have just scanned. There is also a "scan manually" button for you to place your camera in front of the code and wait for the approved scan to take you to the profile of the person.
